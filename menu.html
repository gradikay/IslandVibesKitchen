Jamaica's Culinary Tradition

Jamaican cuisine is a vibrant fusion of African, Indian, European and Chinese influences. Our dishes feature authentic jerk seasoning - a fiery blend of Scotch bonnet peppers, allspice, thyme, and other spices that defines Jamaican cooking.

From farm to table, we honor the traditions of Jamaica where fresh local ingredients create unforgettable flavors.

Dietary Information

At Hilltop Caribbean Grill, we strive to accommodate all dietary needs. Many of our dishes can be modified to be gluten-free, vegetarian, or vegan. Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary restrictions, and our chefs will be happy to prepare something special for you.
