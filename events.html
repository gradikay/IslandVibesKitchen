Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our catering and event services

How far in advance should I book catering services?

+

We recommend booking catering services at least 4-6 weeks in advance for small to medium events, and 2-3 months for larger events (50+ guests) or during peak season (May-September and December). However, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests when possible.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

+

Yes, we can accommodate most dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-specific requests. Please inform us of any dietary needs when planning your event so we can prepare accordingly.

What is included in your catering services?

+

Our standard catering service includes food preparation, delivery, setup, serving equipment, and cleanup. We offer various service levels from drop-off to full-service with staff. Rentals like tables, chairs, linens, and dinnerware can be arranged for an additional fee.

Can I customize my catering menu?

+

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom menus that reflect your preferences and event needs. While we have standard packages, we're happy to work with you to design a menu that perfectly fits your occasion, whether you want traditional Jamaican dishes or fusion options.

Do you provide beverage service?

+

Yes, we offer non-alcoholic beverage packages including our signature tropical fruit punch, sorrel, and ginger beer. For alcoholic beverages, we can provide rum punch and other cocktails where permitted by the venue, or we can work with a licensed bartending service for your event.

What is the minimum guest count for catering?

+

Our catering services are available for events with a minimum of 15 guests. For smaller groups, we offer family-style takeout options that may be more suitable for your needs.
