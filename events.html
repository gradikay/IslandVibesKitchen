Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our catering services

How far in advance should I book catering services?

+

For small events (under 30 guests), we recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance. For larger events like weddings or corporate functions, 1-3 months' notice is ideal, especially during peak seasons (May-September and December). Last-minute requests are accommodated when possible, but availability may be limited.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

+

Absolutely! We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and many other dietary requirements. Please inform us of any allergies or restrictions when booking, and our chefs will create appropriate options without compromising on flavor or presentation.

What is your service area for catering?

+

We service locations within a 30-mile radius of our restaurant without additional travel fees. For locations beyond this radius, a travel fee may apply. For certain large events, we can travel even further - please inquire for specific quotes.

What is included in the catering packages?

+

Our standard catering packages include food preparation, delivery, setup, serving equipment, and eco-friendly disposable plateware. Full-service packages also include professional staff for serving, food management throughout the event, and cleanup. Bar service, premium dinnerware, and decor elements can be added for an additional fee.

Can I customize the menu for my event?

+

Yes! While we offer standard packages, we're happy to work with you to create a custom menu that suits your event's specific needs and preferences. Our chef can design a menu that balances your favorite dishes with appropriate options for all your guests.

Do you offer tastings before booking?

+

Yes, we offer menu tastings for events with 50+ guests. Tastings are scheduled after an initial consultation and include samples of your potential menu selections. For smaller events, we invite you to dine at our restaurant to experience our cuisine before booking.
