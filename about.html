About Us

Our story, our heritage, our flavors

Our Story

Hilltop Caribbean Grill was born from a love of authentic Jamaican cuisine and a desire to share the vibrant flavors of the Caribbean with our community.

Founded in 2005 by the Johnson family, our restaurant brings generations of Jamaican culinary traditions to your table. What started as a small family operation has grown into a beloved local institution, while still maintaining the warm, laid-back island vibe that made us special from day one.

Our name, "Hilltop Caribbean Grill," reflects both our physical location on a small hill and the highland regions of Jamaica known for their incredible cuisine. We create a place where the community can gather to enjoy good food, good music, and good companyâ€”just like in Jamaica.

Our Philosophy

Authentic Flavors

We believe in staying true to the bold, vibrant flavors of Jamaica. Our spice blends and cooking techniques are authentic and traditional, delivering the true taste of the Caribbean with every bite.

Fresh Ingredients

Quality starts with the freshest ingredients. We source locally whenever possible, and import authentic Caribbean spices and products to ensure our dishes have that genuine island taste.

Island Hospitality

At Hilltop Caribbean Grill, you're treated like family. We embrace the warm, relaxed hospitality of Jamaica, creating a space where everyone feels welcome and the stress of everyday life melts away.

Celebrating Jamaican Culture

Jamaica's rich heritage influences everything we do at Hilltop Caribbean Grill. From our vibrant colors to our reggae music and, of course, our authentic flavors, we aim to bring a piece of Jamaica to every guest who walks through our doors.

Our restaurant proudly displays the colors of the Jamaican flag - black, yellow, and green - representing the strength, natural resources, and agriculture of this beautiful island nation.

Our Commitment to Community

We believe in giving back to both our local community and supporting initiatives in Jamaica:

  • Partnering with local farms for fresh produce and meats
  • Supporting Jamaican coffee and spice producers
  • Hosting community events and cultural celebrations
  • Sponsoring educational programs for underprivileged youth
  • Implementing eco-friendly practices in all aspects of our business

The spirit of "One Love" guides our approach to business and community involvement.
