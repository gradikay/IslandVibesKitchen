Jerk Chicken

Our signature dish

Chef's Choice
$15.99

Our signature Jerk Chicken is marinated in our secret blend of authentic Jamaican spices and slow-grilled to perfection. The result is a smoky, spicy, and incredibly flavorful dish that transports you straight to the streets of Kingston.

Each serving comes with our homemade jerk sauce, rice and peas, and a side of fresh festival (sweet Jamaican fried dumplings). The chicken is tender, juicy, and carries the perfect level of heat - spicy enough for authenticity but still enjoyable for most palates.

Signature Dish Spicy Popular

Ingredients

Chicken, scotch bonnet peppers, allspice, thyme, garlic, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, brown sugar, soy sauce, lime juice, and our secret blend of Caribbean spices.

Preparation

Marinated for 24 hours and slow-grilled over pimento wood for authentic smoky flavor.

Heat Level

Medium-spicy (can be adjusted upon request)

Serving Size

Quarter chicken (leg and thigh) or breast, with sides

Chef's Note

"Our jerk chicken recipe has been in my family for generations. The key is in the balance of spices and the slow cooking process. We import many of our ingredients directly from Jamaica to ensure authentic flavor. For the best experience, try it with a side of our homemade scotch bonnet pepper sauce, but be warned – it packs quite a punch!"

- Chef James, Head Chef
